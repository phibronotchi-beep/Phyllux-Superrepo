# Community Guidelines

**Purpose:** Guidelines for community engagement and collaboration in the Phyllux ecosystem.

## Research Community (Tier 4)

**Guidelines:**
- Use qualified language in publications
- Attribute prior art (January 7, 2026)
- Maintain scientific integrity
- Support open source principles

## Commercial Community (Tiers 1-3)

**Guidelines:**
- Respect licensing terms
- Maintain ethical boundaries
- Support mission alignment
- Collaborate effectively

## General Guidelines

**Communication:**
- Professional and respectful
- Qualified language
- Accurate information
- Ethical behavior

**Contributions:**
- Follow licensing terms
- Maintain quality standards
- Support ecosystem goals
- Respect boundaries

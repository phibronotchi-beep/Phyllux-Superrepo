# Recognition Models

**Purpose:** Framework for recognizing contributions to the Phyllux ecosystem.

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

---

## Research Recognition

**Publications:**
- Citation of prior art (January 7, 2026)
- Attribution in publications
- Research collaboration credit

**Code Contributions:**
- Attribution in code
- Contributor recognition
- Open source credits

---

## Commercial Recognition

**Partnerships:**
- Partnership announcements
- Case studies
- Success stories

**Technical Contributions:**
- Technical validation credit
- Integration recognition
- Performance improvements

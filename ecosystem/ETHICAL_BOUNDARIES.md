# Ethical Boundaries

**Purpose:** Ethical guidelines and boundaries for the Phyllux ecosystem.

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

---

## What We Won't License

**Weapons Systems:**
- Military weapons
- Offensive systems
- Harmful applications

**Surveillance Without Consent:**
- Non-consensual surveillance
- Privacy violations
- Unethical monitoring

**Harmful Applications:**
- Technologies that harm individuals
- Applications that violate human rights
- Unethical uses

---

## What We Will License

**Medical Applications:**
- BCIs for medical use
- Neuroprosthetics
- Clinical applications

**Research:**
- Academic research
- Open source projects
- Educational use

**Mission-Aligned Applications:**
- Climate tech
- Social impact
- Positive applications

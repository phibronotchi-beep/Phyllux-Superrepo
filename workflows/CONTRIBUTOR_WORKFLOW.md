# Contributor Workflow

**Purpose:** Process for onboarding contributors and managing contributions to the Phyllux ecosystem.

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

---

## Contributor Types

![Contributor Onboarding](visuals/workflows/contributor-onboarding.svg)

### Research Contributors (Tier 4)
- Academic researchers
- Open source developers
- Educational institutions
- Research organizations

### Commercial Contributors (Tiers 1-3)
- Technology partners
- Commercial licensees
- Strategic collaborators
- Industry partners

---

## Contribution Process

### Research Contributions
1. Access code (MIT license)
2. Conduct research
3. Publish results (with attribution)
4. Contribute back (if desired)

### Commercial Contributions
1. Establish partnership
2. Negotiate licensing terms
3. Execute agreement
4. Begin collaboration

# Trademark Strategy

**Purpose:** Trademark strategy for Phyllux brand and technologies.

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

---

## Trademark Considerations

**Phyllux Brand:**
- "Phyllux" as brand name
- Technology names: Mesh, Wave, Vault, Core
- Method spine: "Phyllotactic" terminology

**Protection Strategy:**
- Consider trademark registration
- Monitor for infringement
- Maintain brand consistency
- Protect against misuse

**Purpose:** Technology Readiness Level (TRL) assessment for core Phyllux technologies and key fusions. Informs commercialization strategy and partnership discussions.

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

## TRL Scale

![Technology Readiness Spectrum](visuals/strategy/technology-readiness-spectrum.svg)

**Standard TRL Definitions:**
1. **Basic principles observed and reported**
2. **Technology concept formulated**
3. **Analytical and experimental proof of concept**
4. **Component validated in laboratory**
5. **Component validated in relevant environment**
6. **System/subsystem model demonstrated in relevant environment**
7. **System prototype demonstrated in operational environment**
8. **System complete and qualified**
9. **Actual system proven through successful mission operations**

**Phyllux Maturity Mapping:**
- Conceptual (1-2/10) → TRL 1-2
- Derived (3-4/10) → TRL 2-3
- Simulated (5-6/10) → TRL 3-4
- Structural (7-8/10) → TRL 4-5
- Candidate (9-10/10) → TRL 5-6

## Core Technologies

### Phyllux Mesh (Phyllotactic Neural Meshing)

**Current TRL:** 4-5 (Component validated in laboratory / Component validated in relevant environment)

**Evidence:**
- ✅ Public code implementation (biomimetic-inventions-public/PNM-public/)
- ✅ Simulation results (crosstalk analysis, SNR improvement)
- ✅ Technical figures (121-electrode layout, spatial uniformity)
- ✅ Prior art established (January 7, 2026)

**Gaps to TRL 6-7:**
- ⏳ In-vivo validation needed
- ⏳ Clinical testing required
- ⏳ Regulatory pathway planning
- ⏳ Manufacturing process development

**Path to TRL 6-7:**
1. Partner with neural interface company (e.g., Neuralink)
2. Conduct in-vivo validation studies
3. Develop manufacturing process
4. Plan regulatory pathway (FDA, etc.)

**Timeline to TRL 6-7:** 12-24 months with partner

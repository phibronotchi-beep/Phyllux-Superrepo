## Competitive Landscape

### Neural Interfaces

**Competitive Advantages:**
- Non-periodic electrode layouts (potential crosstalk reduction)
- Scalable Fibonacci-based counts
- Public prior art (defensive IP)
- Method spine (137.508° golden angle)

**Competitive Challenges:**
- Established players (Neuralink, Synchron)
- Regulatory barriers
- Clinical validation requirements
- Market penetration

**Differentiation:**
- Geometric approach (phyllotactic vs. regular grids)
- Public code and prior art
- Flexible licensing (4-tier model)
- Research collaboration (Tier 4)

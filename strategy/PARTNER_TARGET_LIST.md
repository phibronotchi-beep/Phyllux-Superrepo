## Outreach Sequence

### For Tier 1 (Pioneer)
1. Send brief (post-PPA)
2. Follow up after 2 weeks
3. Schedule technical discussion
4. Negotiate terms
5. Execute agreement

### For Tier 2 (Mission-Aligned)
1. Send brief (post-PPA)
2. Follow up after 3 weeks
3. Schedule mission alignment discussion
4. Negotiate terms (mission discount)
5. Execute agreement

### For Tier 3 (Commercial)
1. Send brief (post-PPA)
2. Follow up after 4 weeks
3. Schedule technical validation
4. Negotiate standard terms
5. Execute agreement

### For Tier 4 (Research)
1. Send research brief
2. Provide code access (MIT license)
3. Support research collaboration
4. Track publications and citations

### Brief Generation
1. AI generates initial draft from template
2. Human reviews and customizes
3. AI checks qualified language
4. Human finalizes and sends

### Opportunity Scoring
1. AI reads fusion data
2. AI scores on dimensions
3. AI updates OPPORTUNITY_MATRIX.md
4. Human reviews and adjusts

### Pipeline Management
1. AI logs conversations
2. AI suggests follow-ups
3. Human reviews and approves
4. AI updates status
